An easy and delicious Carrot Cake

Vegetables in my cake? It is more likely than you think

This Carrot Cake recipe is super easy to make AND it tastes amazing. All your dinner guests will be left asking for more (you have been warned). Do keep in mind that unlike cooking, baking is not an art form. It is a science. This means that the ingredients are pretty set in stone.

Ingredients

Cake Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 ¼ cup
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups grated carrots
  • (Optional) 1 cup chopped pecans

Frosting Ingredients:

  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • (Optional) 1 cup chopped pecans

Baking Directions

  1. Prepare all ingredients and preheat your oven to 350° F (175° C). Grease and flour a 9x13′ pan.
  2. Beat sugar, oil, eggs, and 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract together in a large bowl. Mix till homogeneous.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt to your mix. Stir in carrots (Optionally add pecans here).
  4. Pour mixture into prepared 9x13′ pan.
  5. Bake mixture in oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean (usually about 40 minutes). When cooked take out of the oven and let it sit for 10 minutes. After 10 minutes turn out onto a wire rack and let cool completely.
  6. While the cake cools it's time to make the frosting. Start with beating butter, cream cheese, confectioners' sugar, and 1 teaspoon vanilla extract together in a large bowl till homogeneous and creamy.
  7. Optional, add the rest of the pecans.
  8. Add your frosting to the now cooled cake and enjoy!
