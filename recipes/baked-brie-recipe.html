Wrapped Baked Brie

An impressive starter/snack. Delightful taste. Amazed guests. (Don't tell them it was super easy to make)

This pastry wrapped baked brie will make quite the impression on your guests. Bask in the glory of their praise. Despair that there is so little, perhaps you should have gotten two of them?

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) wheel of Brie cheese
  • 3 tablespoons apricot or peach preserves
  • 1 sheet frozen puff pastry, thawed (you can also make your own if so inclined)
  • 1 egg white

Cooking Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350° F (175° C). Lightly grease a baking sheet or line with parchment paper
  2. Slice the brie in half so that you have two circles of Brie
  3. Spread the apricot or peach preserves on one of the cut halves of the Brie
  4. Place the Brie back together like it was never cut
  5. Wrap the entire wheel of Brie with a sheet of puff pasty. Put wrapped Brie onto prepared baking sheet seam side down. Brush the top of the pastry with egg white
  6. Bake in the oven until the pasty is a golden brown (~30 minutes)
  7. Serve immediately after baking and enjoy!
